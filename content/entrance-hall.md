# Entrance Hall

The grand entrance to the dungeon. Ancient stone walls are covered with moss, and torches flicker in iron sconces.

## Description

A 30-foot square chamber with a vaulted ceiling. The air is damp and cool.

## Exits

- **East**: Passage to the [[treasure-room]]
- **North**: Back to [[example-dungeon-map]]

## Encounters

Roll for random encounters on a d6:
1-3: Empty
4-5: 2d4 Kobolds
6: Gelatinous Cube
