# Treasure Room

A chamber filled with ancient relics and coins scattered across the floor. Mark any changes you make on the [[map.excalidraw|Dungeon Map]] so other parties can keep track of hazards.

## Description

This 25-foot by 20-foot room glitters with gold and jewels. However, something seems amiss...

## Treasure

- 2,000 GP in various coins
- A +1 Longsword (magical aura)
- Potion of Healing (x3)
- Ancient scroll (unidentified)

## Trap

**Pressure Plate**: DC 15 Perception to notice, DC 12 Dexterity to disarm. Triggers poison darts (2d6 damage, DC 13 Constitution save for half).

## Exits

- **West**: Back to [[entrance-hall]]
- **Return**: [[map.excalidraw|Dungeon Map]]
